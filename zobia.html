Information about Zubia Forest Trail

An amazing green spot, the Zubia path is located in the Zubia forests in the north of the city of Irbid, and Zubia is known for its abundance of oak forests and its height above sea level, as it is considered one of the highest heights in the Irbid governorate. For heaven on earth, the length of the path is about 15 kilometers, but the views are overwhelming, so that you feel rather relaxation and not fatigue during the path.
