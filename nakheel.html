Information about Palm Valley

A unique natural spot, the Palm Valley is located near the shobak area, and it extends for more than 18 km, connecting the area with Wadi Araba. The valley has a number of water springs with clear water. Adventurers flock to the valley to explore the surrounding nature of rare plants and birds, walk inside the valley and capture the most beautiful Pictures that are formed by the entry of sunlight between the rocks.
