Information about the Sea of Galilee

The view of Tiberias is located in the north of the city of Irbid, overlooking Lake Kinneret on the edge of the Umm Qais Mountains on a southern elevation of the Golan Heights and Yarmouk and near Lake Kinneret. There is also a group of restaurants, cafes and hotels with wonderful views, in addition to the ruins of the city of Umm Qais. In addition, it offers a charming panoramic view of Lake Kinneret, the Galilee Mountains, the summit of Mount Hermon, the fields of the Jordan Valley, the summit of Tabor, and the Yarmouk River.
